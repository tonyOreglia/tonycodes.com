Creator of Jumble Journal

The world is better when people write more. Jumble is a space for daily flow of thought journaling. It implements the FIRE framework to support sustainable personal growth. Jumble Journal started as an experiment in Zero Knowledge Architecture. Soon it evolved into a full fledged B2C micro-saas product. This product evolved in Cupertino, California at Draper University after being selected from a pitch competition. Jumble went on to be a top Product Hunt launch and is today a profitable company where thousands of journal entries and insights are created daily.

Creator of Air Books

I love reading and collecting books.Air Books enables the sharing economy of physical books by making it effortless to upload your entire bookshelf as a digital bookstore. It does this by streaming images from your phone camera to an OCR pipeline to accurately identify the books on your shelf just from the view of the spines.

This website runs on my home server

This website is proudly hosted on a Lenovo ThinkStation P520 workstation running from my home. The P520 was originally designed for professional workstations but repurposed as a reliable home server. The site itself uses the simplest technology stack suited to the purpose — vanilla HTML, CSS and JavaScript deployed via a simple bash script using rsync. This lightweight approach ensures fast loading times, easy maintenance and low cognitive load. I love the feeling of hosting my personal websites, available to the entire world, from my living room.
